Privacy Policy

Camera To PDF Scanner

Last updated: [2025-12-24]

This Privacy Policy describes how Camera To PDF Scanner ("we", "our", or "the app") handles user information and protects your privacy when you use our Android application.

1. Information We Collect

We respect your privacy. Camera To PDF Scanner does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

All document scanning, image processing, and file creation are performed entirely on your device. No scanned documents, images, or files are uploaded to any server.

2. Camera & Storage Usage

The app requires access to certain device features to function properly:

  • Camera – Used only to scan documents.
  • Storage / Media Access – Used to save scanned documents (PDF or JPEG) on your device and to allow you to select images from your gallery.

These permissions are used strictly for core app functionality and not for data collection.

3. File Storage

Scanned files are saved locally on your device. By default:

  • JPEG files: Internal Storage / Pictures / PdfScanner /
  • PDF files: Stored locally on your device

You have full control over your files and may delete them at any time.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties.

When you choose to share documents (via email, Bluetooth, Google Drive, or social apps), the data is handled by the selected third-party app according to their own privacy policies.

5. Children’s Privacy

Camera To PDF Scanner does not target children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through this app, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

6. Security

All document processing is performed locally on your device. We do not transmit or store your files on external servers, helping to ensure maximum privacy and security.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. You are encouraged to review this policy periodically.
