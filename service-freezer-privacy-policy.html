Privacy Policy

Service Freezer (Root)

Last updated: [2025-12-24]

This Privacy Policy explains how Service Freezer ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects user information when you use our Android application.

1. Information We Collect

Service Freezer is designed to work entirely on the user's device. We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information.

The app may collect limited non-personal data for the following purposes:

  • Crash and performance data using Google Crashlytics or Google Analytics to improve app stability.
  • Advertising ID (ADS_ID), if applicable, used only for advertising or analytics purposes.

All collected data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify individual users.

2. Password & Security

If you enable the password or biometric protection feature:

  • All passwords or authentication data are stored locally on your device only.
  • No passwords are transmitted, uploaded, or stored on any server.

3. Permissions Used

Service Freezer requires certain permissions to function correctly:

  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE – Used only for backup and restore features.
  • Root access – Required to freeze or manage system services and packages.

These permissions are strictly used for app functionality and not for data collection.

4. Root Disclaimer

Service Freezer works only on rooted devices. Rooting your device may void your warranty and can cause system instability. You are solely responsible for any risks associated with rooting your device.

5. Children’s Privacy

Service Freezer does not target or knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately and we will take appropriate action.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share user personal data with third parties.

Anonymous analytics and crash data may be processed by trusted third-party services such as Google Analytics and Google Crashlytics.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page. You are advised to review this policy periodically.
